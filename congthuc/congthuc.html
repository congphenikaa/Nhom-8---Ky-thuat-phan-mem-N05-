TUNA SASHIMI

15 min.
Easy

Sashimi is a Japanese delicacy consisting of fresh raw fish or meat sliced into thin pieces and often eaten with soy sauce.

2 persons

Ingredients

  • 500g sashimi-grade tuna
  • 2 avocados, flesh cut into thick slices
  • Soy sauce for dipping

Tools

  • Sharp knife
  • Cutting board

Directions

  1. Prepare the tuna by slicing it into thin pieces.
  2. Arrange the tuna and avocado slices on a plate.
  3. Serve with soy sauce and enjoy!
